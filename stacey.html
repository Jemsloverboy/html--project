Lindtsey Stacey Aubrey Sevilleja (born July 13, 2003) better known as Stacey,
is a Filipino artist and performer. She is the fifth eldest member,
main rapper and lead dancer of the Filipino girl group BINI.

Profile

    Stacey's favorite color is pink.

    Stacey’s hobby is doing makeup.

    Stacey’s favorite SHA subject is dancing.

    Her special talent is modelling.

    Her favorite motto is ” Always do your best and God will do the rest.”

    One of her popular lines is "Si Jhoanna, amoy santol."

    She is also referred to as "Sining", a song by Dionela featuring Jay R. in which Stacey was featured in the music video for the song.
