BINI Aiah, also known by her real name Maraiah Queen Arceta, is born on January 27, 2001.
She is the oldest and tallest member of the Nation's Girl Group but is known for being the sweetest.
Like her fellow Gen-Z co-stars in the industry, Xyriel Manabat and Francine Diaz, they posses
charm and impeccable talent. Aiah entered as a trainee in Star Hunt Academy in October 2019,
where she was the last to be casted among the girl trainees. Under SHA, she developed her skills in
singing and dancing under Filipino voice coaches Kitchy Molina and Anna Graham, and dance coaches Mickey Perz
and Matthew Almodovar. She has also undergone the intensive Korean training system with the help of MU Doctor Academy
where she learned the Korean practice and discipline in singing and dancing.
